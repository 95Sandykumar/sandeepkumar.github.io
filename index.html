01 / The short story

Data analytics, project management, and a builder's instinct.

At Becker's I own the Snowflake + dbt data platform end to end: a medallion ELT ingesting tens of millions of events, the company's email-validation and provider-classification sources of truth, marketing automation, and a multi-app analytics platform.
